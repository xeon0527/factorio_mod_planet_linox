Version: 0.1.0
Date: 17. 11. 2025
  Info:
    - In the Recursive Blueprints Mod map settings, the "X, Y Coordinates" must be set to "Center"; otherwise, the sample of the first factory site may malfunction.
    - Saves from previous versions will not be properly compatible. To play the game correctly, you must start a new game.
    - To use this version in an ongoing game, you need to temporarily remove the mod to completely clear its data from the save, then add the mod again.

  Major Features:
    - You can now secure your first factory site for free by talking to L.O.R.A.X, and an example automated factory producing iron and copper plates will be generated there.
    - With the example automated factory, various resources and buildings are now provided to ease the early game, significantly reducing the difficulty.
    - The surface and underground power grids are now connected globally from the moment terrain is generated, with no research required.
    - Solar panels can now be used on the surface.
    - Expanded the size of the auxiliary space around factory sites for circuits.

  Changes:
    - Updated L.O.R.A.X's dialogue and images.
    - Updated the Linox planet images.
    - You can now obtain crude oil, sulfuric acid, and useful specialized recipes through research, making self-sufficiency theoretically possible.
    - The power converter has been removed.

  Balancing:
    - Adjusted the stone consumption in the casting stone brick recipe from 1 to 2.
    - Adjusted the number of land plots provided by the Linox rocket silo foundation research from 4 to 2.

  Bugfixes:
    - Fixed an issue where manipulating the GUI after loading a save that was saved while the GUI was open would cause a crash.
    - Fixed an issue where the foundation tiles of Linox installations could be forcibly removed.
